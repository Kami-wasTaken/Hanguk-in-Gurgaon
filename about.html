About “Hanguk in Gurgaon”

This website was created with the purpose to aid Korean expatriates adjust to the Indian lifestyle specifically in Gurugram, Haryana. The purpose of the website is to make you feel at home when living in Gurgaon and to help you interact with locals as well as find your commodoties in Gurgaon.

The Etiquette page has information about the various things you have to be mindful of when interacting with native Indians. By knowing about the beleifs and behaviour of Indians, you will be able to interact with Indians without feeling awkward.

The commodoties page will help you find things like groceries, restaurants, convinient stores and clothing stores that sell Korean commodoties.
